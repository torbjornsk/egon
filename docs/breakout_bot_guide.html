Egon Breakout Bot

N-bar breakout scalping with EMA trend filter and tight trailing stop

Overview

The breakout bot captures momentum moves by entering when price breaks above the highest high (or below the lowest low) of the last N candles. It trades only with the trend using an EMA 9/21 crossover filter, and exits via a tight ATR-based trailing stop that locks in profits while the move continues.

This is a high-frequency, high-volume strategy: it produces many trades with a low win rate (~35%), but winners are significantly larger than losers due to the favorable risk/reward ratio (typically 5:1 or better).

Key Characteristics: Low win rate + high R:R = positive expectancy. This is the opposite of the sniper bot (high WR, tight exits). Expect many small losses followed by occasional large winners. Do not get discouraged by losing streaks.

How It Works

Price Action with Breakout Entry: $2,345 =================== N-bar High (breakout level) /\ | / \ /\ | EMA9 > EMA21 (uptrend confirmed) / \ / \ | $2,335 / \/ \ | \ /\ | \ / \--+--- Price breaks above N-bar high X \ → LONG ENTRY here / \ \ Trail follows price up (0.6 ATR distance) ------------------------------------------------------- $2,320 =================== N-bar Low (short breakout level) EMA9 < EMA21 needed for short

Entry Logic

  1. Calculate the highest high and lowest low of the last N candles (default: 5)
  2. On the current candle close:
    • Long: close > N-bar high AND EMA 9 > EMA 21 (uptrend)
    • Short: close < N-bar low AND EMA 9 < EMA 21 (downtrend)
  3. Check that ATR is above the minimum threshold (dead market filter)
  4. Respect the re-entry cooldown (wait N bars after last breakout signal)

Exit Logic

The breakout bot has no candle-based exit signal. All exits are mechanical:

  • Stop Loss — placed at entry using ATR-based distance (default 1.0x ATR)
  • Trailing Stop — after breakeven is triggered, trails at 0.6x ATR distance
  • Breakeven — SL moves to entry price once profit reaches 0.5x ATR

This means: once a trade is open, the bot either gets stopped out quickly (small loss) or the trailing stop ratchets up behind a strong momentum move (large win). There is no RSI exit, no time-based exit, no profit target percentage.

Configuration Parameters

Breakout Entry

ParameterDefaultDescription
breakout_bars 5 Number of candles to look back for high/low. Lower = more signals, noisier. Higher = fewer signals, stronger breakouts.
breakout_min_atr 2.0 Minimum ATR value (in $ for gold) to allow entries. Filters out dead, low-volatility markets where commission eats profits.
breakout_re_entry_bars 1 Number of bars to wait after a breakout signal before allowing a new entry. Prevents immediate re-entry after a stop-out.

Trailing Stop

ParameterDefaultDescription
breakeven_atr_trigger 0.5 Move SL to entry when profit exceeds this ATR multiple. Lower = faster breakeven (safer but may get stopped out early).
breakeven_offset 0.05 Small offset above entry when moving to breakeven (in ATR). Covers spread to avoid getting stopped at exact entry.
trail_atr_after_breakeven 0.6 Trail distance after breakeven (in ATR multiples). This is tight -- captures momentum while giving the trade room to breathe.
trail_atr_before_breakeven 1.0 Trail distance before breakeven (in ATR multiples). Wider to avoid premature exits on initial volatility.

Stop Loss

ParameterDefaultDescription
atr_multiplier 1.0 Initial stop loss distance in ATR multiples. For breakout, this is tighter than sniper (1.0 vs 1.75) because we want to cut losers fast.
atr_high_volatility_multiplier 0.8 SL multiplier during high-volatility regime. Even tighter during spikes to limit max loss.

Risk Management

ParameterDefaultDescription
risk_per_trade_pct 0.01 (1%) Risk per trade as fraction of account balance. Lower than sniper (2%) because breakout trades more frequently.
breakout_max_daily_loss_pct 0.05 (5%) Maximum allowed daily loss as fraction of starting balance. Bot stops trading for the day when hit.
breakout_max_daily_trades 150 Maximum trades per day. Safety cap to prevent runaway trading during choppy markets.
breakout_max_drawdown_pct 0.15 (15%) Total drawdown limit. Bot shuts down completely when peak-to-trough exceeds this.
max_drawdown_limit 0.15 Used by the core RiskManager. Set equal to breakout_max_drawdown_pct.

Trend Filter

ParameterDefaultDescription
fast_ema 9 Fast EMA period for trend detection.
slow_ema 21 Slow EMA period. When fast > slow = uptrend (longs allowed). When fast < slow = downtrend (shorts allowed).
enable_shorts true Enable short breakout entries. Set to false for long-only mode.

Running the Bot

From the GUI

The breakout bot appears in the Bot Instances list as "Breakout Scalper (Pip Scalper)". Click to open its detail panel, edit parameters, and press Start.

Standalone (Command Line)

.venv\Scripts\python.exe run_breakout.py
.venv\Scripts\python.exe run_breakout.py --config config/breakout_params.json
.venv\Scripts\python.exe run_breakout.py --config config/my_custom_breakout.json

Creating Custom Variants

Copy config/breakout_params.json and adjust parameters:

  • Wider breakout (fewer trades, stronger signals): increase breakout_bars to 8-12
  • Tighter trailing (lock profits faster): reduce trail_atr_after_breakeven to 0.4
  • More aggressive (more trades): reduce breakout_min_atr to 1.5, set breakout_re_entry_bars to 0
  • Higher timeframe (H1 breakouts): set "timeframe": "H1" and increase breakout_bars to 10-20

Each variant needs a unique magic_number and bot_label.

How It Fits In

The breakout bot uses the standard Egon architecture:

  • BreakoutStrategy implements the TradingStrategy protocol (entry/exit only)
  • It plugs into BaseTradingBot which handles: connection, candle detection, trailing stop, breakeven, profit protection, MT5 close detection, position sizing
  • Registered in BOT_REGISTRY as 'breakout' with magic number 234300
  • Works with both MT5Broker (live) and SimBroker (backtest) without modification

Tuning Guide

When Win Rate Is Too Low (<25%)

  • Increase breakout_bars (stronger breakout = fewer false signals)
  • Increase breakout_min_atr (skip dead markets)
  • Try a higher timeframe (M15 instead of M5)

When Winning Trades Are Cut Short

  • Increase trail_atr_after_breakeven (give winners more room)
  • Increase breakeven_atr_trigger (delay breakeven activation)

When Losses Are Too Large

  • Decrease atr_multiplier (tighter initial SL)
  • Decrease risk_per_trade_pct (smaller position size)

When Too Many Trades Per Day

  • Increase breakout_re_entry_bars to 2-3
  • Increase breakout_bars (fewer breakout signals)
  • Lower breakout_max_daily_trades
Backtest first: Always run a backtest comparison before changing parameters on a live bot. Use tests/run_comparison.py to A/B test configs. Win rate alone is misleading -- focus on total P/L and max drawdown.

Risk Warnings

False breakouts: In choppy, range-bound markets the breakout bot will produce many false signals. The tight stop loss limits damage, but expect losing streaks of 10-15 trades. The daily loss limit (5%) is your safety net.
Spread sensitivity: With high trade frequency, spread cost is significant. The default config uses market orders (not limit). At $0.30 spread per trade and 50 trades/day, that is $15/day in spread costs. Ensure your R:R compensates for this overhead.
Correlation with sniper: If running both bots simultaneously, be aware they may both enter on the same momentum move (sniper on the RSI extreme that caused the breakout). Monitor total exposure across all bots.

Origin

Inspired by the "Pip Scalper" breakout concept. Original optimized parameters (M5, June 2026):

  • Breakout bars: 5
  • SL: 5 pips (~$0.50 for gold at 10:1, mapped to 1.0x ATR in Egon)
  • Trail: 3 pips (~$0.30, mapped to 0.6x ATR)
  • Min ATR: $2.0
  • Re-entry bars: 1
  • Reference backtest: 372 trades, 34.9% WR, +$304, R:R 5.70, DD 0.7%
