Egon Pip Scalper

Breakout entry with instant profit-locking and tight ATR trailing

How It Works

The Pip Scalper captures momentum breakouts and immediately protects profit. The philosophy is simple: never give back gains. The moment a trade goes positive, the stop loss moves to breakeven. From there, a tight trailing stop follows price every second, locking in more profit as the move continues.

Pip Scalper Trade Lifecycle: Each candle close: calculate breakout levels from last N candles | | Place BUY STOP at breakout_high + 0.1*ATR (with SL at breakout_high) | Place SELL STOP at breakout_low - 0.1*ATR (with SL at breakout_low) | v Price touches BUY STOP level → MT5 fills instantly (zero latency) | | Position open, SL at breakout level (failed breakout = small loss) | v Price moves in your direction | | Trailing stop updates every 100ms (configurable) | SL follows: current_price - 0.4*ATR (tight trail after BE) | v Price reverses, hits trailing SL | | Exit with locked profit | v 3-bar cooldown, then new stop orders placed on next candle

Entry Logic

The bot uses MT5 pending stop orders for instant breakout fills:

  1. Each candle close: compute the N-bar high and low
  2. Place a BUY STOP slightly above the high (buffer = 0.1 x ATR)
  3. Place a SELL STOP slightly below the low (buffer = 0.1 x ATR)
  4. MT5 fills the order server-side the instant price touches it
  5. When one fills, the opposite side is cancelled
  6. Orders are cancelled and replaced each candle as levels shift

Trend filter: BUY STOP only placed when EMA 9 > EMA 21 (uptrend). SELL STOP only when EMA 9 < EMA 21 (downtrend).

Additional filters:

  • ATR filter: No orders placed when ATR is below minimum (dead market)
  • Re-entry cooldown: After a fill, waits N bars before placing new orders

Why Stop Orders?

With Python polling (even at 1-second intervals), price can move $4+ between detection and order execution on XAUUSD M1. MT5 stop orders execute at the broker with zero latency — the moment price touches the level, you're in. The entry price equals the stop price exactly (minus any slippage).

Exit Logic

The Pip Scalper has no candle-based exit signals. All exits are mechanical:

  1. Stop loss (initial or trailing) hit by MT5
  2. Take profit hit (if configured, acts as max-profit cap)
  3. Profit protection (optional, time-based tightening)

This means the trailing stop IS the exit strategy. The tighter the trail, the smaller and more frequent your wins. The wider the trail, the larger but less frequent your wins.

How Trailing Works (Tick by Tick)

Example: BUY STOP filled at $2,340.00 (breakout high was $2,339.50), ATR = $5.00 Tick 0: Fill @ $2,340.00 SL = $2,339.50 (at breakout level) 100ms: Price = $2,340.20 SL -> $2,340.75 (BE + 0.15*ATR offset) [LOCKED] 200ms: Price = $2,340.80 Trail = $2,340.80 - 0.4*ATR = $2,338.80 But $2,338.80 < $2,340.75, so SL stays at $2,340.75 500ms: Price = $2,342.00 Trail = $2,342.00 - 2.00 = $2,340.00 < BE, stays 1.0s: Price = $2,343.50 Trail = $2,343.50 - 2.00 = $2,341.50 [NEW HIGH] 2.0s: Price = $2,345.00 Trail = $2,345.00 - 2.00 = $2,343.00 3.0s: Price = $2,344.00 SL stays at $2,343.00 (never moves down) 3.5s: Price = $2,342.90 SL $2,343.00 HIT -> EXIT with +$3.00 profit
Important: The trail distance is relative to ATR. On volatile days (ATR = $8), the trail will be $4.00 wide. On quiet days (ATR = $3), it will be $1.50. This automatically adapts to market conditions.

Running the Bot

Standalone

.venv\Scripts\python.exe run_breakout.py --config config/breakout_params.json

From GUI

The bot appears in the instance list as BRK. Start it from there and configure via the config editor panel.

Custom Config

Copy config/breakout_params.json, change the magic_number and bot_label, adjust parameters. The GUI will auto-discover it.

Tuning Guide

Too many stop losses?

  • Increase breakout_bars (e.g., 7-10) for stronger breakouts
  • Increase breakout_min_atr to filter weak signals in quiet markets
  • Increase breakout_sl_atr_mult for more initial room
  • Enable schedule to avoid volatile news windows

Winning trades are too small?

  • Increase trail_atr_after_breakeven (e.g., 0.8-1.0) to let winners run longer
  • Switch to breakeven_mode: atr_threshold with breakeven_atr_trigger: 0.5 to avoid getting stopped at BE on noise

Too few trades?

  • Decrease breakout_bars (e.g., 3) for more frequent breakouts
  • Decrease reentry_cooldown_bars to 1-2
  • Decrease breakout_re_entry_bars to 1

Getting chopped in ranging markets?

  • Enable volatility guard (vg_enabled: true) to pause during ATR spikes
  • Enable schedule to only trade active sessions
  • Increase breakout_min_atr to skip dead market periods
Risk Warning: The pip scalper on M1 can generate many trades per day. At 1% risk per trade with 100 max daily trades, the theoretical maximum daily loss is significant. In practice, the loss backoff and daily loss cap prevent this, but always monitor the bot during its first live sessions.
